CCDE

•考試科目：CCDE™ Written Exam 352-001 & Practical Exam 352-011

•認證方式：

第一步：CCDE筆試

You must pass the two-hour, written qualification exam, which covers advanced networking infrastructure design principles and concepts.

Once you pass the Written Exam, you are eligible to schedule the Practical Exam.

第二步：CCDE上機實測

The eight-hour Practical Exam is a scenario-based exam that will assess your ability to translate business strategies

into a flexible network infrastructure that combines data, storage, voice and video.

•報考資格：無

•Exam Topics：

Written Exam : 352-001(120 minutes 90-110 questions)
Practical Exam : 352-011(480 minutes)

*Candidates will be required to answer each question before moving on to the next question.

In other words, candidates will no longer be allowed to skip a question and come back to it at a later time.

•認證方式：

第一步：CCDE筆試

You must pass the two-hour, written qualification exam which covers networking concepts and some equipment commands

before you are eligible to schedule the lab exam.

第二步：CCDE上機實測

The eight-hour lab exam tests your ability to configure actual equipment and troubleshoot the network in a timed test situation.

You must make an initial attempt of the CCIE lab exam within 18 months of passing the CCIE written exam.

If you do not pass the lab exam within three years of passing the written exam, you must retake the written exam

before being allowed to attempt the lab exam again.

•報考資格：無，但建議有3~5年相關工作經驗。

•Exam Topics：

*Candidates will be required to answer each question before moving on to the next question.

In other words, candidates will no longer be allowed to skip a question and come back to it at a later time.





CCA

•認證方式：

第一步：Application and Interview

Applicants for CCAr are required to submit a resume (subject to verification), a summary of an architecture project,

and a non-refundable fee of US$ 3,750. Architect Board members, appointed by Cisco, will interview and evaluate applicants to decide

whether they are qualified to proceed to the in-person Board Review. Certification is a selective process and not all applicants are

expected to qualify for the Board Review.

第二步：Board Review

Once qualified by the Architect Board, candidates must submit their final payment of US$11,250 to obtain the architecture challenge

materials and schedule their live Board.

•報考資格：

CCA認證不僅花費昂貴，而且也極不容易通過。 CCA認證需要花費15000美元。報考者不僅必須擁有一個CCIE或CCDE認證，

並且需要有至少10年的相關行業的工作經驗。報考者必須提出申請，然而再經過思科的嚴格篩選才能參加培訓。

一旦被允許參加培訓課程，報考者必須為一系列商業需求提出一個架構解決方案。屆時，考試委員會將會對培訓者進行質詢，

培訓者則需要對方案進行答辯。
